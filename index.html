About Vaikhari

The Philosophy Behind the Name

Vaikhari holds profound significance in Hindu and yogic philosophy. It represents the last of four stages that sounds, or "sabda," travel through as they become perceptible and audible to our senses. It is considered the gross-level articulated sound of living beings and physical objects.

Our Initiative

The Vaikhari initiative is dedicated to creating simple, intuitive applications that help users across all ages to:

  • Increase productivity through focused work practices
  • Learn and develop new skills
  • Achieve their personal and professional goals
  • Experience technology that respects their time and attention

Just as Vaikhari represents the articulation of sound into its most tangible form, our apps translate the philosophy of productivity and learning into practical, user-friendly tools that make a real difference in everyday life.
