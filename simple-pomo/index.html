About Simple Pomo

Simple Pomo is a Pomodoro timer application designed to help users improve their productivity through time management. The app features a sprint timer with customizable short and long breaks, allowing users to track their work sessions locally on their device.

A clean and distraction-free Pomodoro timer to help you master your focus and productivity.

  • Focused work sprints
  • Regular break intervals
  • Progress tracking
  • Customizable timers
  • Minimalist design

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Simple Pomo ("we," "us," "our," or the "App"). At Vaikhari Apps, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains our policies and practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

Simple Pomo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Personal identifiers
  • Browsing or usage history
  • Biometric information
  • Financial information

Your sprint records, timer settings, and app preferences are stored exclusively on your device's local storage and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. Permissions

Storage Permission

Simple Pomo requests Storage permission to save your sprint records and timer data locally on your device. This permission allows the app to:

  • Save your sprint history and timer settings
  • Retrieve your saved data when you open the app
  • Export or backup your data if such features are added in the future

All data remains on your device and is never uploaded to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Simple Pomo does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external data processors. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage services for user data
  • Payment processors (no in-app purchases or subscriptions)

5. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

All data generated by Simple Pomo is stored exclusively on your device. We do not maintain servers that store user data, and no information is transmitted over the internet.

Data Security

While we do not collect data, we encourage you to:

  • Keep your device updated with the latest security patches
  • Use a secure lock method (PIN, password, or biometric) to protect your device
  • Regularly back up your device to prevent data loss

6. Data Retention

Since Simple Pomo does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no data retention policy applicable to remote storage. Data stored locally on your device remains there until you delete the app or manually remove the files.

7. User Rights

Since Simple Pomo does not collect personal data, the following rights are not applicable in the traditional sense:

  • Access: You have complete access to all your data stored locally on your device
  • Deletion: You can delete your data by uninstalling the app or manually clearing app data through your device settings
  • Portability: Your local data remains on your device and can be backed up using your device's native backup tools

If you have specific requests regarding your data, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Simple Pomo does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Our app is designed for general productivity use across all age groups. Since we do not collect personal information, we do not distinguish between users of different ages. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise their children's device usage.

9. International Compliance

GDPR Compliance

Simple Pomo is compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as it does not collect, process, or store any personal data of users within the European Union or elsewhere.

CCPA Compliance

Simple Pomo complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as it does not sell, share, or process personal information of California residents or users worldwide.

Global Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Simple Pomo, regardless of their location. Our privacy practices are designed to be compliant with privacy laws in all jurisdictions where the app is available.
